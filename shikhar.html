Shikar



Kanpur Institute of Technology organized its 11th Annual Sports Meet-‘SHIKHAR’-18 on 16th & 17th February 2018. Students from various colleges of AKTU participated with full enthusiasm in various indoor and outdoor sports events. The objective of this event was not only to enhance the sports skills of students but also to encourage the competitive spirit and positive zeal between the students of different institutions. Various colleges across Kanpur - PSIT, DAV, DBS, Rama University, Naraina, Axis, Allenhouse etc; participated in the event and made it successful. The event concluded with prize distribution ceremony and the winners were awarded with trophies, certificates and cash prizes.
