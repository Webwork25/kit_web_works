    About Kanpur Institute of Technology EN Department

    The primary objective of the department has been to impart quality education, training and research at the undergraduate, postgraduate levels in various areas of Electronics and Communication Engineering. The major areas of faculty expertise of the department include Analog & Digital Communication Systems, Control Systems, Micro processors, Digital Signal Processing, RF, Satellite and Microwaves, Speech Signal Processing, Embedded & VLSI design and Wireless communication. The Department is headed by Dr. Nitin Trivedi, with the team having blend of young and experienced faculty members. Electronics & Communication Engineering Department has Accreditation of NBA granted by Engineering & Technology Accreditation Evaluation committee In House student forum of Institute of Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering (IETE) Kanpur Chapter at KIT. Courses Offered : 4 Years B.Tech Course – Seats In take – 180 to 60 M.Tech (EC) – Seats Intake – 18 Placed Students : Working various PSU’s and private enterprises such as BHEL, NTPC, ONGC, IOCL, BEL, HAL, DRDO, ISRO, BARC, WIPRO, INFOSYS, TCS, ERICSSION, NOKIA-SIEMENS, ALCATEL-LUCENT, FLEXTRONIC, BHARTI TELECOMM, NIIT, GENERAL ELECTRICALS, SONIC ,BSNL and other prestigious organization. Affiliations: AKTU, Lucknow and AICTE, New Delhi
